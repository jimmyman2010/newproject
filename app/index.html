Slim Line Rock Panels

Owing to popular demand Norstone has introduced a new SlimLine (SL) stone cladding range specifically designed for budget constraints.

Available in Ochre (oh-ker) and Charcoal colours only, our new Slim Line is slightly thinner and lighter and allows us to pass significant savings on to our clients. They are the same natural stone as our IL and XL ranges and the same size (610mm x 152mm x 15-30mm) panels but weigh only approx. 48 kilos per m2.
