Basalt Panels & Tiles

Our Basalt 3D Panels and Basalt Inter-Locking tiles are perfect for a sophisticated modern design. Made from cooled Basaltic lava rock cut and honed handles all the elements, water salt, chlorine, heat and is perfect for pools, water features, feature walls, fireplaces, kitchen backsplash, bathrooms, exterior / internal stone wall cladding.
